L.A.M.P. Stack Development

Build powerful, cost-effective web applications on the industry's most proven open-source stack. Linux, Apache, MySQL, and PHP power millions of websites worldwide — and our team knows every layer.

Advantages

Why L.A.M.P.?

Battle-Tested Reliability

The L.A.M.P. stack has powered the web for over two decades. From startups to enterprise platforms, its stability and maturity reduce risk and ensure long-term maintainability.

Cost-Effective & Open Source

Every layer of the stack is free and open-source. No licensing fees, no vendor lock-in. You own your infrastructure and benefit from a massive ecosystem of community-driven tools and extensions.

Massive Talent Pool

PHP and MySQL are among the most widely known technologies in the world. Finding developers, documentation, and community support is effortless, keeping your project agile and scalable.

What We Do

Our L.A.M.P. Services

Custom Web Applications

Bespoke PHP applications built from scratch — CMS platforms, portals, dashboards, and SaaS products tailored to your business logic.

Server Administration

Linux server setup, hardening, and ongoing management. Apache tuning, SSL configuration, firewall rules, and performance optimization.

Database Design & Tuning

MySQL schema design, query optimization, indexing strategies, replication setup, and migration from legacy databases to modern structures.

Legacy Modernization

Upgrade aging PHP codebases to modern standards — PHP 8+, Composer-based architecture, PSR compliance, and automated testing integration.

How We Work

Our Process

01

Discovery & Architecture
