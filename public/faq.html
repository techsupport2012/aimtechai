Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with AIM Tech AI. If you cannot find your answer here, do not hesitate to reach out.

Category

General

What does AIM Tech AI do?

AIM Tech AI is a full-service software & AI development and technology consulting company. We specialize in AI and machine learning, cloud infrastructure, UI/UX design, quality assurance, and custom software & AI development for businesses of all sizes.

Where are you located?

Our headquarters is in Beverly Hills, California. We operate as a remote-first company with over 100 team members distributed across multiple time zones, allowing us to serve clients worldwide.

How long have you been in business?

AIM Tech AI was founded in 2022. Since then, we have grown to a team of over 100 employees and have delivered hundreds of successful projects across a wide range of industries.

What industries do you serve?

We work with clients across healthcare, finance, e-commerce, education, real estate, entertainment, and more. Our solutions are tailored to the unique requirements and compliance standards of each industry.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy. Book a free consultation call or email us at info@aimtechai.com. We will discuss your goals, scope out the project, and provide a detailed proposal within days.

Category

Services & Process

What services do you offer?

We offer AI and machine learning solutions, technology consulting, UI/UX design, cloud infrastructure management, quality assurance and testing, and end-to-end custom software & AI development. Visit our Services page for full details.

How does your development process work?

We follow an agile methodology with two-week sprint cycles. Every project begins with a discovery phase, followed by design, development, testing, and deployment. You receive regular updates and demos throughout the entire process.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. We offer flexible maintenance and support plans after launch, including bug fixes, performance monitoring, feature enhancements, and 24/7 critical incident response for enterprise clients.

What technologies do you use?

Our stack spans modern frameworks and platforms including React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, TypeScript, AWS, Azure, GCP, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Docker, Kubernetes, and more. We choose the best tools for each project's needs.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We have deep experience integrating with legacy systems, third-party APIs, and existing infrastructure. Our team will assess your current architecture and build solutions that work seamlessly alongside what you already have.

Category

Pricing & Timeline

How much does a project cost?

Project costs vary based on scope, complexity, and timeline. We provide detailed, transparent proposals after an initial discovery session. Whether you need a small MVP or an enterprise platform, we have solutions for every budget.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines depend on the project scope. A simple MVP can be delivered in 4 to 8 weeks, while larger enterprise solutions may take 3 to 6 months. We provide realistic timelines during the proposal phase and keep you updated at every milestone.

Do you offer fixed-price contracts?

Yes. We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials contracts depending on the nature of the project. Fixed-price works well for clearly defined scopes, while time-and-materials provides flexibility for evolving requirements.

What are your payment terms?

We typically structure payments in milestones tied to project deliverables. A deposit is required to begin work, with subsequent payments due upon completion of agreed-upon phases. We accept wire transfers, ACH, and major credit cards.
